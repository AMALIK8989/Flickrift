EuroTrip : 2004

Description

When Scott and his high school lover break up after graduation, he travels to Europe to search for his pen pal, Mieke, after angering her. What begins as a series of misadventures soon turns into an unforgettable journey of self-discovery, friendship, and young love. Starring Scott Mechlowicz, Jacob Pitts, Michelle Trachtenberg, Travis Wester, Jessica Boehrs, and Matt Damon. Directed by Jeff Schaffer. A fun and raunchy comedy that captures the spirit of youthful adventure and the chaos of traveling abroad
